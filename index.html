ABOUT US

Reheat Aero is the new name for the business that was previously known as Reheat International.

With a history going back to 1998, Reheat is a leading independent MRO and aftermarket provider offering a one-stop shop solution for a wide range of aircraft galley equipment and cabin interior products. Our specialty covers equipment manufactured by all of the major galley and interior OEM’s.

Reheat is able to provide:

  • Component repairs for many ATA Chpt. 25 and 38 parts from our CAA, FAA and TCCA part 145 approved repair station.

  • Parts sales from our comprehensive inventory of new condition spare parts.
  • Rotable sales, exchanges and loans from our extensive stock of serviceable ovens, oven control modules, microwaves, coffee makers, water boilers, toilet water heaters, faucets and cockpit door strikes.
  • New product sales and distribution on behalf of OEM principles.
  • Consignment partnerships offering a facility that can store, repair, certify and onward sell parts from your surplus inventory or aircraft tear down projects.

We operate from a modern 16,000 sq ft MRO facility with workshops, warehouse and offices located in Alton, Hampshire, UK only 20 minutes drive from Farnborough Airport, 40 minutes drive from London Heathrow airport and within 1 hours from London Gatwick, Luton and Stansted airports.

Our extensive experience, focus and knowledge has been used to create a company that fully understand the needs of customers in the aviation industry and aims to meet these needs by concentrating solely on core competencies and working hard to achieve the highest technical and customer service standards.

Reheat Aero specialise in providing repair services for galley insert equipment manufactured by all the major interior OEMs including Adams Rite Aerospace, Collins Aerospace, Safran, Goodrich Aerospace, Iacobucci, IPECO, JAMCO, MAPCO and others.

Reheat can provide repairs from our CAA, FAA and TCCA Part 145 approved repair station and has capabilities for dual release on equipment within ATA Chapters 25 and 38.

Our maintenance philosophy revolves around building in reliability and quality with every repair to help reduce overall cost of ownership. We specialise in those parts and our experienced and well-trained technicians repair these products day in day out providing highly skilled and reliable fault finding and rectification. Working from a modern, well equipped workshop facility with ongoing investment in inventory to ensure we have the right parts on the shelf, customers can be sure of receiving the very best level of service and support.

With a capability list of over 2500 part numbers, Reheat can offer real one stop shop aftermarket support, allowing customers to deal with a single galley insert MRO capable of assisting with repairs, spares and exchanges across all part numbers and manufacturers.

Several flexible support solutions are available to meet customers needs including simple ad hoc time and materials, fixed pricing, or a fully comprehensive Rate per Flying Hour support agreement.

Vendor Consolidation

As part of a comprehensive one stop shop solution Reheat can assist customers with reducing their vendor base by consolidating repairs, parts purchasing and exchange pool support through us as a specialist in this area.

PARTS SALES

Reheat has a significant on-site inventory of new condition OEM spare parts that are used in both our repair workshops but also available for sale to airlines and MROs with their own in-house capability.

From O Rings to motor assemblies you can find these listed on ILS or simply make an enquiry with us and all parts are provided with original OEM certification and traceability. If we have it in stock, we can offer you quick delivery with minimal order quantity or value requirements.

Rotables Sales, Exchanges & Loans.

Reheat holds a very large rotable inventory which is available for sale, exchange or loan. Most of this inventory consists of ovens (convection and steam), microwaves, coffee and beverage makers, water boilers, toilet water heaters, liquid containers & hot cups, faucet assemblies and cockpit door strikes. 

Rotables will be provided in SV or OH condition with a fresh CAA / FAA airworthiness release from our own workshop and will come with our comprehensive standard warranty.

Advance or standard exchanges are available from our exchange pool and to meet short term inventory shortages we can arrange loans on very reasonable terms.

We can accept credit card payments for Parts Sales

SALES &
DISTRIBUTION

Using our extensive airline and aftermarket customer network we can provide OEMs with an effective route to market.

Our warehouse can stock and distribute new parts quickly and effectively especially for the UK, European and Middle East markets. 

Should you be looking for a pro-active and well-connected marketing and distribution partner / reseller for your interior products please get in touch.

CONSIGNMENT PARTNERSHIPS

If you are an OEM, airline or aftermarket provider with surplus galley equipment related parts or material we can help you. 

We hold and help sell OEM surplus parts from manufacturing overruns, cancelled orders or returns. 

We also work with airlines who want to sell surplus parts from aircraft no longer in service or left over from cabin refurbishment / upgrade programmes. 

If you are involved in an aircraft tear down or part out and want a partner who can store, inspect, repair, certify and sell on the galley equipment we are experienced in doing this.
